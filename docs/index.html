About the Course

This course is designed by industry professionals with over 12 years of experience in banking and financial risk management. It combines theoretical knowledge with practical insights to provide a comprehensive understanding of financial risk management.

Target Audience

This course is specifically designed for professionals in banking roles such as:

  • Senior Tech Business Analysts
  • Business System Analysts
  • Project Managers
  • Data Analysts

Learning Objectives

By the end of this course, you will be able to:

  • Understand the fundamental concepts and methodologies of market risk, credit risk, and counterparty credit risk
  • Apply risk measurement techniques to real-world scenarios
  • Interpret risk metrics and use them to inform business decisions
  • Navigate regulatory requirements and compliance challenges
  • Implement risk management strategies in banking systems and processes
