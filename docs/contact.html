Frequently Asked Questions

How can I get technical support for the course?

For technical support, please email support@financialriskmanagement.com with a detailed description of your issue. Our support team will respond within 24 business hours. Include screenshots or error messages if applicable to help us resolve your issue more quickly.

Can I request additional content or topics?

Yes! We welcome suggestions for new content. Please use the contact form and select "Content Suggestion" as the subject. Provide details about the topic you'd like to see covered, and our content team will review your request.

How do I report errors or inaccuracies in the course material?

If you find any errors or inaccuracies in our course material, please let us know by using the contact form with the subject "Content Error Report." Specify the module, section, and describe the issue in detail. We appreciate your help in maintaining the quality of our content.

Are there opportunities to contribute to the course?

We occasionally invite industry experts to contribute to our course content. If you're interested in contributing, please send your resume and a brief proposal to contributors@financialriskmanagement.com. We're particularly interested in practical case studies and emerging risk management techniques.
